Our Purpose

Make public transport more accessible by connect South African taxi commuters to information about shared minibus taxi routes, fares, and locations in different areas.

Approach

TaxiRoute will use crowdsourcing to obtain data about shared minibus taxi routes, fares, and locations in different areas. The data will also be sourced from official channels within the taxi industry. This means that the data can be populated and updated more frequently.
